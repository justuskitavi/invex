Welcome to Invex

What is Invex?

Invex is an Inventory, Expense, and Employee Management Web Application built with Django. It enables small to medium-sized shop owners to manage their operations efficiently with powerful tools for sales tracking, employee records, stock monitoring, and more.

Core Features

  • ✅ Multi-shop support for each user
  • ✅ Smart inventory tracking with threshold alerts
  • ✅ Employee management: Add, fire, and monitor staff
  • ✅ Daily sales reports by email
  • ✅ Secure authentication with OTP verification
  • ✅ Password reset/change via email
